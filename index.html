Gummy Bear Collector

Collect the perfect amount of gummy bears!

  • You will be given a random Gummy Score at the start of the game.
  • Above the scores are four gummy bears. By clicking on a gummy bear, you will add a specific number of points to Your Score.
  • To win the game, match your score to the Gummy Score.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the gummy bears and the Gummy Score.
