Gummy Bear Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four gummy bears below. By clicking on a gummy bear, you will add a specific number of points to your score.

To win the game, match your total score to the number above the gummy bears.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the gummy bears.
