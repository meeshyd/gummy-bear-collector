Gummy Bear Collector

Collect the perfect amount of gummy bears!

  • You will be given a random Gummy Bear Score at the start of the game.
  • Below the Gummy Bear Score are four gummy bears below. By clicking on a gummy bear, you will add a specific number of points to your score.
  • To win the game, match your score to the Gummy Bear Score.
  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the gummy bears and the Gummy Bear Score.
